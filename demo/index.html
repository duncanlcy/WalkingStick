Waist Safety Pad (Hub)

Primary data collector, fall/impact detection, BLE peripheral.

  • MPU6050/BMI160 IMU on I2C (SDA 21, SCL 22)
  • Piezo buzzer on GPIO 25 for critical alerts
  • In-memory ring buffer (64 samples) — SD card hooks reserved
  • Advertises WALKING_STICK_SERVICE_UUID
